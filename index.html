Features & advantages

Diversified Social Modes
PINs combines multiple elements such as gaming, content creation, and dramatized socializing. This diversified social pattern, differing from traditional pure social modes, brings additional entertainment value, information value, and a new form of content creation value to users.
GitHub of the Social Field
Similar to GitHub, new participants in PINs can create based on historical content. The economic mechanism of PINs will ensure the economic interests of all creators. PINs will also adopt a DAO mechanism to manage the Verse.
Cryptographic Social Platform Tailored for Generation Z
Generation Z is no longer satisfied with traditional and outdated social forms like Facebook. They desire new things and trends that belong to their era. PINs is a social platform that can showcase their uniqueness or individuality.
Multi-Chain Aggregated Social Platform
PINs can aggregate and support NFTs and tokens on multiple public chains, allowing these NFTs and tokens to play a role in PINs' dramatized socializing. NFTs and tokens generated in PINs' economic mechanism can also be extracted to these public chains.

Token（ERC20）
The PINS Token (ERC20) is the universal currency of the PINS world. It can be used to pay for NFT props and currency assets in different Verses, and invest in different Community DAOs to obtain returns, among other functions.
