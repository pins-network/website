Unique Aspects

Technical Foundation
PINs Network is built on the EMIT Core protocol, an elastic blockchain infrastructure based on Block Lattice, designed for DApps. It operates through various DApps, such as roles constructor, DAO, multimedia data container, and more.

Token（ERC20）
The PINS Token (ERC20) is the universal currency of the PINS world. It can be used to pay for NFT props and currency assets in different Verses, and invest in different Community DAOs to obtain returns, among other functions.
